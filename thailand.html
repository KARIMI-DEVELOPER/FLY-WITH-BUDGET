THAILAND

Visitor Visa (Tourist Visa):
1. Many nationalities are eligible for a visa exemption, allowing them to stay in Thailand for up to 30 days without obtaining a visa in advance. However, this can vary, and the length of stay may differ for some nationalities.
2. Tourist Visa: For those not eligible for visa exemption or planning to stay longer, a tourist visa can be obtained in advance from a Thai embassy or consulate.
