SINGAPORE

Visitor Visa (Tourist Visa):
1. For many nationalities, there is no need to apply for a visa in advance if the stay is for tourism purposes and does not exceed a certain period (e.g., 30 or 90 days).
2. A valid passport with at least six months of validity beyond the intended stay.
3. Proof of sufficient funds for the duration of your stay.
4. Confirmed return or onward ticket.
