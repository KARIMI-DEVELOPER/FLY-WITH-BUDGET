ITALY

Visitor Visa (Tourist Visa):
1. A valid passport with at least three months of validity beyond your intended departure.
2. Completed visa application form.
3. Passport-sized photos.
4. Travel itinerary, including flight reservations.
5. Proof of accommodation (hotel reservation or letter of invitation).
6. Proof of financial means to cover your stay (bank statements, sponsorship letter, etc.).
7. Travel insurance covering at least 30,000 euros of medical expenses.
8. Proof of ties to your home country (employment, property, family).
9. Visa fee.
