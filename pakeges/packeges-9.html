Lets go on an unforgetable journey to Quetta and Ziarat with our exclusive tour packege.Experience the beauty of Bab e Ziarat, Quaid e Azam Residency, Sandeman Tangi and many more. Ziarat is a hill resort 120km (2 hours 30 minutes) drive from Quetta. The transition of landscape from dry, barren, rugged mountains to lush green mountains clad with juniper trees is dramatic. This area has the second largest juniper tree jungle in the world.
