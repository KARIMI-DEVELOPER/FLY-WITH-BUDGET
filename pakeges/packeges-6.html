Kumrat Valley is situated in District Upper Dir KPK Pakistan & adjacent to Swat Valley and Chital Kalash Valley. Kumrat Valley and Swat Kohistan are connected via Badgoye Pass, while Kalash Valley is connected through Kashken Top. The Badgoye Top is also a Picnic Spot and containing Outsized and Lime Parklands. Jeep track is used to travel between Utror and Thall Kumrat Valley, which is very less explored due to many reasons as bad road condition, lack of basic facilities and disinterest of Govt and Political Leaders. The valley is Lush Green with many Meadows & full of trees. The Kumrat Valley is the last border of Dir Kohistan.
